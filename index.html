Frequently Asked Questions

What if I need something in particular cleaned?

No problem! Just let us know in advance or on the day which item/s you wish for us to pay particular attention to.

I live in CITY NAME, can you clean my home/office?

We serve a large part of the Greater Boston area, if you have doubts, please get in touch and we'll let you know.

Will I have the same cleaners each week?

We try to be as accomodating with your requests as we can. Though we have a flexible team, this may not always be possible.

Do I need to provide anything?

We bring our own supplies and gear. If there is a special product you already have that you would like us to use, just let us know!

How often should my home be cleaned?

As much as you like, or as much as you need! Everybody has different needs so if your space is regularly kept up, you may need us left often.
